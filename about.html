The value of a Chicken Nugget?

Infinite

The world is full of beauty; and while poets, prophets, and philosophers have rightly claimed that the creations of God exceed the creation of man in every way, there is one case in which man seems to nearly touch the divine...The Chicken Nugget

Many have argued that this is the food of children, to be given at McDonald's in a happy meal or to satisfy the hungry mobs at your kid's birthday party, they have so much value beyond what is seen by parents. Children understand the simple things in life. There is a reason Jesus asked children to come unto Him, because they understand what is right and haven't been corrupted by the world. This is why their taste in breaded chicken is so necessary for us to understand, because they see what we do not...happiness in simplicity.

The world has too many complex problems which divide us, and chicken nuggets are the solution: they break down barriers between old and young, liberal and conservative, and leave out only the snobs who think they're too good for them. (We wouldn't want them in our society anyways, as they're the ones that cause all the problems) With a society built on chicken nuggets we would have no conflict in Russia, Ukraine, Palestine, Israel, Africa, etc. We could unite the world in appreciation of a simple truth: that chicken nuggets are amazing.
